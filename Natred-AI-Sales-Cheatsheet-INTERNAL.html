AI Sales Cheat Sheet

How to talk about Natred's AI services — what we sell, who it's for, and how to explain it.
The big picture: We build custom AI solutions that automate the boring, repetitive work the client's team does every day. We're not selling a product off a shelf — we scope it to their business and build it for them. Every solution connects to their existing software. The key message: we make their team more productive without changing how they work.
01

Automate the Admin

On flyer as "Automate the Admin"
What It Actually Is
Document processing automation. AI reads invoices, forms, delivery notes, patient intake sheets — any structured document — and pushes the data straight into their business system (Sage, Xero, a CRM, spreadsheets, whatever they use).
What Problem It Solves
Their admin staff spend hours every day manually typing data from paper or PDF documents into their systems. It's slow, error-prone, and expensive. This eliminates that entirely.
Who It's For
Any business with an admin team doing manual data capture: medical practices (patient forms), accounting firms (invoices), logistics (delivery notes), retail (supplier docs), property management (lease docs).
What They Save
Hours of admin time per day. Reduced data entry errors. Staff freed up to do actual valuable work instead of typing numbers into boxes.
Example Scenario
"Your accounts team receives 50 supplier invoices a week by email. Right now someone opens each one, reads the amounts, and types them into Sage. We set up AI to read every invoice as it arrives, extract the data, and enter it automatically. Your person just reviews and approves."
How to Say It in a Meeting
"How much time does your team spend on manual data capture? We can set up AI to handle that automatically — it reads the documents and puts the data where it needs to go. Your team just checks and approves."
02

Handle Enquiries Automatically

On flyer as "Handle Enquiries Automatically"
What It Actually Is
A smart AI assistant (chatbot or email responder) that answers the repetitive questions a business gets every day. It knows the business, its services, pricing, hours, policies — and responds instantly via their website, WhatsApp, or email.
What Problem It Solves
Their receptionist or admin answers the same 20 questions all day — "what are your hours?", "how much does X cost?", "where do I pay?" This frees that person up to do real work while customers still get instant answers.
Who It's For
Service businesses with high enquiry volume: medical/dental practices, law firms, estate agents, salons, gyms, any business with a front desk or inbox that's constantly busy.
What They Save
Receptionist/admin time. Faster response to leads (no more "we'll get back to you"). Fewer missed enquiries after hours. Better customer experience.
Example Scenario
"A dental practice gets 30+ calls and WhatsApps a day asking about pricing, availability, and how to book. We build an AI assistant that handles those instantly — books appointments, answers FAQs, and only puts through the calls that actually need a human."
How to Say It in a Meeting
"What are the questions your front desk answers most often? We can build a smart assistant that handles those automatically — on your website, WhatsApp, or email — so your team only deals with the things that actually need a person."

03

Turn Your Data Into Answers

On flyer as "Turn Your Data Into Answers"
What It Actually Is
Natural language business intelligence. We connect AI to their business data (databases, spreadsheets, accounting software) so anyone in the business can ask questions in plain English and get real answers — no reports, no dashboards, no waiting for IT.
What Problem It Solves
Decision-makers need answers from their data but have to wait for someone to build a report, or dig through spreadsheets themselves. This gives them instant access to their own information.
Who It's For
Business owners and execs who want faster access to their numbers: MDs, finance directors, operations managers, sales leaders. Especially businesses with data spread across multiple systems.
What They Save
Time waiting for reports. Dependency on IT/finance to pull numbers. Bad decisions made on gut feel because the data was too hard to get to.
Example Scenario
"A managing director wants to know last month's revenue by branch. Right now they email finance and wait two days. With this, they type 'what was revenue per branch last month?' and get the answer in seconds — pulled directly from their accounting system."
How to Say It in a Meeting
"If you could ask your business data any question and get an instant answer — no spreadsheets, no waiting for reports — what would you ask first? That's what we build."
04

Built Around Your Existing Tools

On flyer as "Built Around Your Existing Tools"
What This Really Means
This isn't a product — it's our approach. Every AI solution we build plugs into the client's existing software. We don't ask them to switch systems, learn new platforms, or buy new tools. It's an objection handler, not a line item.
Why It Matters
Most businesses have been burned by IT projects that forced them onto new systems. They're scared of disruption. This message says: "We work with what you have. Zero upheaval."
How to Say It in a Meeting
"We don't ask you to change anything about how you work. Whatever software you're already using — we connect to it. You won't need to retrain your team or learn a new system."

Common Objections & How to Handle Them

"We're not ready for AI."
That's fine — this isn't about being on the cutting edge. It's about automating the tasks your team already hates doing. If someone on your team spends hours on data entry or answering the same questions, you're ready.
"Isn't AI expensive?"
We scope it to your needs and budget. A document automation setup or a smart assistant isn't a six-figure project. Compare the cost to what you're paying in admin hours every month — it usually pays for itself quickly.
"What about data privacy / security?"
Your data stays in your systems. We build solutions that process data securely within your environment. We follow best practices and can work within your compliance requirements — POPIA, industry-specific regulations, all of it.
"We've had bad experiences with IT projects before."
That's exactly why we start small. We'll build one automation, prove it works, and go from there. No massive upfront commitment, no 6-month project timelines. You see results fast or we haven't done our job.
